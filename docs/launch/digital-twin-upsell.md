# Bonus upsell concept

## Recommended bundle

Bundle the e-book with an **Amodei-Bot** at either:

- `$79` for the book plus the chat companion
- `$147` for the book, chat companion, and future interview updates

## Product concept

The Amodei-Bot should be a narrow RAG product over:

- the e-book PDF
- the interview corpus behind it
- major public essays such as *Machines of Loving Grace*
- any future interviews you add as update packs

## Core features

- ask-answer chat with source citations
- mode presets like `Biology`, `Safety`, `Geopolitics`, and `Work`
- one-click prompts such as `What changed between 2024 and 2026?`
- premium answers that end with `What this means for founders` or `What this means for researchers`
- a `Contradictions` view that highlights tension across interviews instead of pretending the corpus is perfectly uniform

## Why it converts

The e-book is a static asset. The bot makes the product feel alive, current, and reusable. That matters for a premium niche audience because they do not just want to read once. They want to interrogate the thesis, extract talking points, and reuse the material in decisions, content, and research.

## Recommended stack

- embeddings + vector store over interview chunks
- citation-first answer formatting
- gated access after purchase
- optional yearly update plan for new corpus additions
