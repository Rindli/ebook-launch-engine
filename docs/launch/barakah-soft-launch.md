# Wealth with Barakah soft launch

## Best first audiences

- practicing Muslims interested in self-development
- Muslim founders and freelancers
- people in Islamic study circles who also care about practical life issues
- family-oriented readers who want a cleaner framework for money
- friends who already talk about business, discipline, or provision in a faith-conscious way

## Good opening angle

Do not sell this as `how to get rich`.

Sell it as:

- a clean Islamic wealth framework
- a reflective synthesis on rizq, halal work, and barakah
- a compact guide for earning without losing spiritual direction

## DM example

I put together a short e-book called **Wealth with Barakah**. It is a synthesis of 13 talks on halal earning, rizq, tawakkul, gratitude, generosity, and family responsibility. The reason I think it may be useful is that it avoids both extremes: guilt around money and empty hustle culture. If you want, I can send you the link.
