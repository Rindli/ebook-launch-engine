# Soft launch kit

This file is the practical launch pack for the first 7 days of selling **The Quiet Exponential**.

## Launch goal

Get the first 10-25 buyers from warm and semi-warm audiences before widening traffic.

## Best first audiences

1. Founder and operator friends who already buy books, courses, or niche reports.
2. AI-curious friends who follow frontier AI but do not want to watch every interview.
3. LinkedIn contacts in tech, product, biotech, investing, and policy.
4. People who have already reacted to your AI posts or discussions.
5. Small private group chats where thoughtful, premium information products can travel by trust.

## 7-day rollout

### Day 1

- Publish the landing page.
- Test checkout and download yourself.
- Send 10 private DMs to warm contacts.

### Day 2

- Post 1 LinkedIn post.
- Post 1 X thread or short text post.
- Send 5 more DMs to people who are especially likely to care about AI safety, Anthropic, or biotech.

### Day 3

- Publish 1 short video from the `traffic-breadcrumb-shorts.md` file.
- Repost the offer with a different angle: biology instead of safety.
- Ask 2-3 trusted people for a reaction or testimonial after reading the first chapter.

### Day 4

- Send a short follow-up to non-responders from Day 1.
- Publish 1 post built around the `country of geniuses` idea.

### Day 5

- Post another short video.
- Send the offer to a small WhatsApp, Telegram, or Messenger group if you have a relevant one.

### Day 6

- Publish one post focused on the `work after intelligence` angle.
- Collect objections people have sent you and turn them into FAQ content.

### Day 7

- Repost with social proof, even if the proof is small.
- Example: `A few smart readers already bought this because they wanted one clean synthesis instead of 20 fragmented interviews.`

## Daily metrics to track manually

- landing page visits
- checkout clicks
- completed purchases
- DMs sent
- replies received
- posts published
- which angle got the strongest reaction: biology, safety, or work

## First benchmark

If 10-20 warm DMs produce zero sales, the problem is usually one of these:

- the ask is too vague
- the landing page is too abstract
- the audience is too cold
- the offer sounds like a generic PDF instead of a premium insight product

If 10-20 warm DMs produce 2-5 sales, that is a healthy early signal.
