# Manual launch checklist

## Before sending traffic

- confirm landing page opens
- confirm checkout button opens Stripe
- confirm paid test leads to success page
- confirm PDF download works from success page
- confirm MailerLite buyer group sync remains enabled

## Before each outreach wave

- decide the angle: biology, safety, or work
- choose 10-20 people only
- personalize the first line when possible
- do not send the same message to everyone

## After each outreach wave

- log who replied
- log who clicked
- log who bought
- save the best objections and best reactions
- reuse the message angles that got replies

## What not to do in the first week

- do not launch to everyone at once
- do not spend on ads before warm sales exist
- do not change price every day
- do not overcomplicate with too many bonuses

## Success condition for week 1

- first 5-10 sales
- first 2-3 strong reactions or testimonials
- one message angle that clearly outperforms the others
